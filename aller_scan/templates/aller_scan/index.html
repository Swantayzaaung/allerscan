Get Food Information

Allerscan is a web app that allows a user to take pictures of their packaged food's nutrition labels and find out what food items may have a potential risk of allergy. It aids doctors in discovering safety solutions and preventions to a problem that could easily go unnoticed.

Our mission

To educate, inform, help and promote healthy diet among doctors, so they could easily use this application to find related information on particular food by scanning its ingredients labels.
